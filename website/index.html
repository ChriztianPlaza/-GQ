The GQ Story

Since 2004, GQ Barbershop has been the premier destination for discerning men hairstyle in Batangas City who demand nothing but the finest in grooming excellence.

Founded by Michel Pineda, our shop combines time-honored barbering traditions with contemporary techniques and premium products. We believe that every man and women deserves to look and feel his absolute best in their men hairstyle. The shop also offers hand and foot care services that our customers love, catering to both men and women.
